Academics

If you ask Brookside graduates what they value most about their time here,
as often as not they’ll say something like, "Brookside taught me how to think.”
Supported by a dedicated faculty, Bates students learn to explore broadly
and deeply, to cross disciplines, and to question rigorously.

At Brookside, students and faculty form a community of scholars who share a thirst
for learning, drawing on the methods of the sciences, the patterns of logic
and language, the study of societies, and expression in the arts.

A Brookside education fosters intellectual inquiry and reflection, personal growth,
and a commitment to the world beyond oneself. Bates offers students a rigorous
academic experience in a collaborative and supportive environment.
