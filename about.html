Founded in 2023, Brookside University is a co-educational, state-assisted, comprehensive, regional, four-year university in Greenwood, S.C. With approximately 4,300 students, Brookside provides exceptional educational opportunities within its traditional liberal arts and science programs, and its professional programs in business, education and nursing.

Mission and Outlook

Brooks has always stood firmly for the ideals of academic excellence, intellectual curiosity, egalitarianism, social justice and freedom.
